What is survival analysis?¶

Statistical methods to analyze data with time to event outcomes

  • Survival: Probability of survival
  • Hazard: Rate of "death" (or event occurrence)
  • Regression: Relationship of factors with event outcome

Survival curves¶

In [27]:
import lifelines

top_qtr_draftees = draft_data[draft_data.draft_pick < 0.25]

plt.figure()
ax = plt.subplot(111)

kmf = lifelines.KaplanMeierFitter()
kmf.fit(top_qtr_draftees["matches"], event_observed=top_qtr_draftees["inactive"], label='Top 25% of draft')
kmf.plot(ax=ax)
plt.xlabel('MLS Match Appearances', fontweight='bold')
plt.ylabel('Proportion of Draftees Remaining', fontweight='bold')
